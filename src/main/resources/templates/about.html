LisenUp!

And Make It Contagious!

If you are the kind of person who knows what they want and willing to improve things by telling whomever is responsible - "Don't you get it?" Then you've come to the right place!

Imagine the day when you can instantly reach those people and companies that are doing these irritating things and influence them with your feedback? That would make a world of difference!

The problem is that these people don't even know they are doing something wrong, so they are not looking for your feedback. To make them notice we need a movement so contagious that they will have no other choice but to join in. So we started LisenUp to become that movement.

We believe that if enough of us show by example, that candid feedback matters, then it will become a norm. A norm is something everyone will want to participate in, including those who today think they don't need it. And these are the people that you want to improve.

If you believe in our mission - then join us, and start to LisenUp and make it contagious!

And in case you are wondering: I'm Vitaliy and I LisenUp!
