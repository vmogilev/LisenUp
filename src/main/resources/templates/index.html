LisenUp is an anonymous feedback engine.

Have you ever prepared a meal and then asked someone if they liked it? Do you think the answer you got was genuine? Probably not ;) Because the person giving you this untruthful answer didn't want to hurt your feelings. In their mind, they were doing you a favor! However, in reality, they were doing you a huge disservice - because now, that mediocre meal became your baseline.

LisenUp fixes this problem by never revealing the identity of your reviewer - giving them complete anonymity. So now, instead of asking someone what they think on the spot, just give them a link to your LisenUp feedback page. Then, this person will give you an honest feedback, at a later moment, when they had time to really think about it and are not concerned with hurting your feelings.
