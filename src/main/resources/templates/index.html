CEOs Within Reach

Only CEOs can change the culture of a company, yet it's the customers and employees that know what's really broken. LisenUp bridges this gap and puts CEOs within reach.

The way it works is very simple. It all starts with you - the CEO, because to grow your company you should do things that don't scale. One of such things is to Delight your customers. And what could be a better way than paying them for their feedback?

That's right! Show your customers and employees that you truly value their opinion by naming a price for their honest and valuable feedback and LisenUp will handle the rest. If the feedback you receive is worthy - simply mark it as such and we'll process the payment on your behalf.

And don't worry, we figured out a way to protect you from the dreaded SPAM. LisenUp has a SPAM-Bond - which is the amount of money you tell is to collect prior to letting anyone provide you their feedback. And if the feedback you receive is SPAMMY - the person that sent you this feedback looses the money they put up for the SPAM-Bond.
